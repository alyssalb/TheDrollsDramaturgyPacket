Welcome

This zine explores the theatrical form of drollery that thrived under Puritan rule in 17th-century England. Navigate each section to discover more.

What is a Droll?

Drolls were short, comedic sketches derived from longer plays. Click below to learn more.

Often staged in secret, drolls were performed in inns and private homes when public theatre was banned.
